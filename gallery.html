Achievements in Health and Well-being in Tamil Nadu Tamil Nadu has made remarkable progress in healthcare and well-being, becoming one of India's leading states in medical infrastructure, maternal and child care, and disease control. The state has significantly reduced infant and maternal mortality rates through initiatives like the Chief Minister’s Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme and the expansion of primary healthcare centers. Efforts in tackling non-communicable diseases, mental health awareness, and emergency medical services have further strengthened the healthcare system. Additionally, Tamil Nadu’s investment in medical research and digital health services continues to improve accessibility and quality of care for all.
