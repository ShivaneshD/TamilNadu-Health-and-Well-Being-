Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme (CMCHIS)

The Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme (CHIS) is a flagship initiative by the Tamil Nadu Government aimed at providing financial protection against catastrophic health expenditure for economically disadvantaged families.

Key Features

  • Free treatment up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year.
  • Coverage for over 1,000+ medical procedures.
  • Cashless treatment available at empaneled hospitals.
  • Pre-existing diseases covered from day one.

Eligibility Criteria

The scheme primarily targets families below the poverty line and other vulnerable groups. Beneficiaries need a valid ration card and must meet the government’s prescribed income criteria.

How to Apply

Eligible individuals can apply through the official government portal or visit the nearest enrollment center with necessary documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Ration Card
  • Address Proof

More Information

For official guidelines and application details, visit the CMCHIS Government Portal.
