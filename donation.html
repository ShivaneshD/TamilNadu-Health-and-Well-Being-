Donation

Donating your eyes is a selfless act of kindness that allows you to live on through the gift of sight. Imagine the joy of someone seeing the world for the first time, witnessing the beauty of a sunrise, or reading a loved one's face—all because of your generosity. Your eyes can become a beacon of hope, transforming darkness into light for someone in need. The Tamil Nadu government has been a pioneer in eye donation through initiatives like the Tamil Nadu State Blindness Control Society (TNSBCS) and Dr. MGR Eye Bank Network, promoting awareness and facilitating corneal transplants. With well-structured eye donation programs, Tamil Nadu has consistently led the way in reducing blindness and ensuring that no donated eye goes to waste. In this fleeting life, the greatest legacy we can leave behind is one of love and compassion. By pledging to donate your eyes through these government initiatives, you not only restore vision but also contribute to a movement that uplifts countless lives. Be the light in someone's world—donate your eyes and let your vision shine on forever!

  • Adolescent health education and counseling.
  • Nutritional guidance and support.
  • Immunization services.
  • Referral services for specialized care.
